About the practice

Architecture rooted in Africa, shaped for a global audience.

MB Architects Limited is a multidisciplinary design and consulting firm headquartered in Lagos, with regional studios in Abuja and Abidjan.

We operate at the intersection of design, business, and social transformation — delivering work that is commercially intelligent, culturally grounded, and socially meaningful. Since our founding, we have completed over seventy commissions across residential, urban housing, hospitality, commercial, and institutional architecture, shaping skylines and communities across twelve countries.

What we hold to

Five commitments that shape every brief.

01

Thoughtful Design

Design that listens before it speaks. Every project begins with a rigorous understanding of place, purpose, and people.

02

Signature Quality

We pursue timeless architecture with a clear identity, rooted in excellence rather than trend.

03

Craftsmanship

Buildings must be made well to live well. We value material integrity, construction intelligence, and the dignity of making.

04

Precision

Architecture is both art and science. We value discipline, accuracy, and technical rigour as foundations of quality.

05

Genuineness

Architecture grounded in context, culture, and honesty. We resist spectacle in favour of meaning so that what we build belongs where it stands.

Sustainable Shelter

A commitment to homes that respect climate, culture, and craft.

Across Nigeria and West Africa, the most urgent design question is shelter: affordable, durable, and rooted in place. We treat sustainable housing as a discipline of its own — prototypes, partnerships, and projects that turn scarcity into dignified, climate-aware homes.

Our Leadership

Leadership Profile

CEO & Executive MD

A visionary architect and entrepreneur, Moyosore Bamigbade holds double Master’s degrees in Architecture and Business of Architecture, and an MBA in Real Estate and Finance from the London School of Economics. He has led multidisciplinary teams in major cities around the world, including Lagos, Abuja, Accra, Banjul, Abidjan, Dakar, Porto Novo, Freetown, Johannesburg, Dubai, London, and Milan.

His leadership has guided projects across three continents, combining African authenticity with global design intelligence.

He is also the Executive Chairman of MB Holdings, where he oversees strategic investments across architecture, construction, real estate, energy, and hospitality.
